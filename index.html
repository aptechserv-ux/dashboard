⚠️ Known Limitations
  • Markapuram & Polavaram — newly formed districts; parent district values used as proxy where own data is unavailable.
  • CCTNS Forms/FIR — inflated for small units. Capped at 15.
  • ASR & Polavaram Dial 112 RT — hilly terrain (verified). Capped at 30 min for scoring equity.
  • MedLEaPR MLC% — hospital non-adoption, not unit negligence.
  • eSakshya SID% — till-date %; units clearing backlog.
  • Dial 112 — GRP units have no control rooms; assigned neutral (50).
  • No crime outcome data — scoring reflects technology adoption only.
